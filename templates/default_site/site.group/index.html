We're Getting Married!

After almost 9 years we both knew enough was enough and it was time to tie the knot! As much as we love attending weddings we knew they weren't for us, so we've decided we're eloping to New York!

The Proposal

Saturday 28th September 2013

After a lovely walk through the grounds of Wollaton park with Archie we arrived at the back overlooking the lake. After a quick dash into the woods I reappeared, got down on one knee and popped the question. A short "Yes" later and we toasted the occation with a picknik and glass of bubbly.

Las Vegas

Thursday 30th January 2014

Just over 4 months later and we'll touch down in Las Vegas for a brief 4 day stop in "The Gambling Capital of the World". We'll be checking out the strip and all it's Casinos and will probably take in a show or two.

Our Big Day

Wednesday 5th February 2014

On February 5th 2014 we'll share our vows and wed at the City Clerk's Office in Manhatten. We'll celebrate our first day as married couple taking in some of the sights and sounds of NY, whilest admiring the views from the Top of the Rock at the Rockefeller Center.

Knees Up!

It wouldn't be a wedding without all our friends and family so we're inviting you to Ruddington Grange Golf club on the evening of Saturday February 8th 2014 to help up celebrate our new chapter together.
